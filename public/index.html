OnForte

OnForte is a crowd-sourcing, platform-crossing playlist creation service, integrating Soundcloud, Spotify, and Apple Music with voting algorithms to create intelligent playlists.

At Your Service

Soundcloud

We are able to offer Soundcloud's free service to all of our users.

Spotify

If the host of the playlist is logged in to Spotify Premium, users can add Spotify music to playlists.

Apple Music

The host is able to add their local music to the playlist. Apple Music APIs have been released but are still undocumented, this should be truly coming soon.

Voting

Express yourself through votes to have an impact on the playlist order.
